# Iran Revolution 2026 - Research Notes

> **Last Updated:** January 25, 2026  
> **Status:** Ongoing situation with severe information blackout

---

## Executive Summary

As of early 2026, Iran is experiencing widespread protests and internal upheaval—referred to by some as an uprising or revolution—that began in late December 2025 following a severe economic crisis and a sharp decline in the value of the rial. The crackdown by Iranian authorities has resulted in significant casualties and mass detentions.

## Nature of Atrocities

### Weapons & Tactics
- **Live fire orders** - Security forces ordered to "shoot to kill"
- **Heavy weapons** - DShK heavy machine guns used against crowds
- **Mutilation tactics** - Intentional targeting of eyes, heads, and genitals
- **Chemical weapons** - Reports of use beginning January 17, 2026

### Hospital Attacks
- Security forces assaulted hospitals (Imam Khomeini, Sina Hospitals) to arrest or kill injured protesters

### Detention Abuses
- Widespread **torture and sexual violence** against detainees
- "Suspicious injections" administered to detainees
- Extrajudicial executions

### Coercion of Families
- Families charged for bullets used to kill their relatives
- Pressured to falsely claim victims were "Basij members"

## Regime Response & Propaganda

### Information Control
- **Near-total internet blackout** since January 8, 2026
- Designed to hide crackdown from outside world

### Legal Persecution
- Protesters labeled as "rioters" and "moharebeh" (waging war against God)
- Judiciary announced "no leniency"

### Disinformation Campaign
**Case Study: Amirhesam Khodayarifard**
- 21-year-old protester shot by security forces in Kuhdasht
- Government falsely claimed he was a Basij member killed by "rioters"
- Family publicly refuted the government's manipulated narrative

## Political Shift

The protests have evolved from economic grievances to demanding:
- **Downfall of the Islamic Republic**
- Support for alternative systems, including potential return to monarchical model
- Discussions about political transition concerning Supreme Leader Khamenei
